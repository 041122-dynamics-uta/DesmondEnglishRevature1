The Good Life

Each of five neighborhood dogs (Saber, Ginger, Nutmeg, Pepper, and Bear) is enjoying one of the following activities: 
1. getting its ears scratched
2. playing catch
3. taking a nap
4. burying a chew toy
5. and going for a walk.


* 		Pepper is either playing catch or burying a chew toy.(2,4) 
* 		Neither Ginger nor Saber nor Bear is on a walk.(1-4)
* 		One of the dogs named after a spice is getting its ears scratched.(1)
* 		A dog not named for a spice is playing catch.(saber, bear)
* 		Bear is getting some exercise.(2,4,5)

Can you figure out what each pooch is doing?Â 

# Answer 

1. getting its ears scratched, (ginger)
2. playing catch (bear)
3. taking a nap (saber)
4. burying a chew toy (pepper)
5. and going for a walk. (nutmeg)
